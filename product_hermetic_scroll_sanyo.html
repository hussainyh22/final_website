Description

Black R22 Sanyo compressor for air conditions



Sanyo C-SB scroll compressor is an electric compressor with internal high and low pressure type totally enclosed structure, and the upper part becomes high pressure and the lower part becomes low pressure by high and low pressure partition. The Sanyo compressor is vertical, with the motor on the lower side and the compressor on the upper side. The electric motor is fixed with the shell hot mount, the upper and lower bearings are welded to the shell, and the upper bearing is fixed with the compressor part (fixed scroll, dynamic scroll, etc.). In addition, the lead wire of the motor through the shell part (terminal block) uses a glass-sealed airtight terminal.

Sanyo refrigeration compressor Freon collection method

  1. Open the air conditioner, in the case of Sanyo scroll compressor work, unscrew the hexagonal cap of the large and small tubes of the air conditioner.
  2. Find the corresponding hexagonal cap, first close the valve of the small tube, and then close the valve of the large tube after more than 10 seconds.
  3. To ensure that the Sanyo compressor speed is normal, the two valves are closed, and then stop the air conditioner can be.
