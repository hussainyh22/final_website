About Pioneer Marine Refrigeration and HVAC

About Pioneer Marine Refrigeration and HVAC

Pioneer Marine Refrigeration and HVAC, established in 2022 and headquartered in the bustling city of Mumbai, Maharashtra, India, is a leading manufacturer and service provider in the field of marine refrigeration and HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) solutions. With a steadfast commitment to excellence, our company has rapidly gained prominence as a trusted partner in the maritime industry.

Services Offered On:

  • Rigs
  • Platforms
  • Oil Tankers
  • Cargo Vessels
  • Supply/Support Vessels
  • Barges
  • Cruise Ships

We render the following services:

  • Designs, fabrication, installation, and commissioning of refits and conversions of HVAC and refrigeration units
  • Complete dry dock solutions in HVAC and refrigeration units for electrical and PLC systems
  • Preventive maintenance services to regulate parameters at optimum operating conditions
  • Fabrication and assembling of high-quality Condensing units with copper tubes and copper and aluminum fins
  • Fabrication of Air Handling units prolonging the lifespan of Condensers and Evaporators for marine use
  • Overhauling of Reciprocating, hermetic, semi-hermetic, and Screw compressors
  • Inspection of the area, design, and fabrication of a high-quality duct system
  • Robotic Cleaning of Square ducts, Manual cleaning of Kitchen Galley round accommodation ducts

Pioneer Marine Refrigeration and HVAC is committed to innovation, quality, and environmental responsibility. We understand the critical role our products and services play in the maritime industry, and we strive to provide sustainable solutions that contribute to the well-being of both the marine industry and the environment.

As we continue to grow and expand our offerings, we remain steadfast in our commitment to providing unparalleled customer service and cutting-edge technology to meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Our extensive products are designed to meet the demanding needs of refrigeration and air conditioning systems, ensuring efficient and reliable performance even in challenging marine environments.

Our skilled technicians and engineers are dedicated to ensuring the optimal functioning of your systems, minimizing downtime, and maximizing efficiency.

Pioneer Marine Refrigeration and HVAC is your trusted partner for all your marine refrigeration and HVAC requirements, delivering excellence on every voyage.
