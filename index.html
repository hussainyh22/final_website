About Us

Since our inception, PIONEER marine refrigeration and HVAC has been at the forefront of the Marine and Offshore HVAC sector, especially in the Mumbai High field. Our growth story has been remarkable, spanning the width of the Indian Peninsula, and consistently delivering top-notch HVAC solutions. Our expertise isn't just in servicing but also in creating. We specialize in the design, fabrication, and commissioning of marine-specific air conditioning systems, chillers, and air-handling units. Through our expertise we cater to the unique needs of marine environments with precision and reliability.
