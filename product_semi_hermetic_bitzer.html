BITZER compressor common faults and their maintenance methods:

  • Winding short circuit, break and winding touch shell grounding:

    • this type of fault are caused by the motor part of the compressor, the base fault now urgent break for the power supply is normal, the compressor does not work: short circuit and touch shell when the power after the protector action, or burn the fuse; it should be noted that if the winding turns between the slight short circuit, the compressor is still able to work, but the working current is very high, the compressor temperature is very high, after a short time, the the thermal protector will act. Winding short circuit and winding touch the chassis grounding can generally be checked with a multimeter; winding short circuit, especially a slight short circuit, because the resistance of the winding itself is very small, so it is not easy to determine, should be based on the measurement of current to determine.

  • Suction and exhaust valves are not closed:

    • If the suction and exhaust valves of the compressor are damaged, even if the refrigerant is sufficient, the system cannot establish high and low pressure or it is difficult to establish qualified high and low pressure, the system is not cooling or the cooling effect is very poor.

  • Holding shaft, card cylinder:

    • compressor if the loss of oil or impurities into the often cause holding shaft or card cylinder, the failure phenomenon is, after the power compressor does not run, the protector action.

  • Vibration and noise:

    • this type of problem often occurs in the maintenance work, the general performance of refrigeration does not have much impact, but will make the user feel abnormal, caused by the cause is often the pipe and the case touch, the compressor's fixed bolt loose and shock absorbing block off.

  • The compressor is an accessory to the thermal protector, and the fault is usually a broken circuit or a small action temperature point. Breakage will cause the compressor does not work;

    • action temperature point is small will cause the compressor to work for a period of time on the shutdown and repeatedly so, the problem is often easy and winding between the turns of a slight short circuit mixed clear, the difference is that the thermal protector is damaged when the working current is normal, winding short circuit current is large.

Refueling precautions:

  • Polyester oil BSE170 has strong hygroscopicity, moisture in the oil and oil chemical reaction, and can not be removed by evacuation work requires special care to minimize the air into the system and oil storage containers

  • The replacement of the compressor should be noted that the original refrigerant has a certain amount of refrigeration oil, if necessary, the system should be blown out of the stored oil, such as playing a large amount of oil exists in the system in the start-up risk of liquid shock.

  • Do not replace the refrigerant oil in the rainy day.
